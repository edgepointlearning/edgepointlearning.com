We offer planning and learning program development, creating templates using commercially-available, industry-standard toolsets, training on how to use those tools, and coaching and support services to teach you best practices.

Are you eager to create your own eLearning or aiming to enhance your team’s existing skills? Let us help you get to the next level. Leverage our vast experience and expertise to help you reach your goals. Whether you're a beginner in the realm of eLearning or seeking to up your game, we are here to provide guidance and support every step of the way.

Our boot camp offerings meet you where you’re at and get you where you want to go. We can start with a high level overview of the eLearning process or jump in where you’re already comfortable.

A custom set of templates

We'll start with your existing branding guidelines, fonts, colors, and ideas, and create an easy-to-use set of templates for your department to leverage for eLearning projects. We want to ensure you stay on-brand, but still have room for creativity and future growth.

An introduction to tools

Once your template is set, we'll teach you the tools of the trade. With an average of 15+ years of experience, we know the ropes and are well-versed in industry standard software.

Mentoring with our pros

When you're ready to fly solo, we'll still be around if you need us. We’re happy to guide you through your first project, offer feedback and advice, and help out if you get stuck.

Best practices

We can also teach you best practices in project management, instructional design, project planning, timeline development, and project processes. We empower you to effectively manage projects, create engaging instructional materials, plan timelines, and execute projects with efficiency and success.
