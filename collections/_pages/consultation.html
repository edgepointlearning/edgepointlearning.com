PinPoint, Your Microlearning Solution

Meet PinPoint Workforce

The future of mobile learning in the flow of work is just a tap away. Connect your frontline to learning with our mobile native platform. PinPoint's precision microlearning surrounds your workplace with learning that taps into the true potential of the smartphone.

How PinPoint Works

Leveraging tools like geolocation, augmented reality, Bluetooth beacons, and bar and QR codes, your microlearning and performance support materials are pushed directly to your employees' smartphones right where and when they need support.

Explore The Admin App

Empower your frontline supervisors with the ability to create and deploy microlearning and performance support tools from any device. Learn how to create assets, set up checklists, and assign training with our guide to the PinPoint admin interface.
